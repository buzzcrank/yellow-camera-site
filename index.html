Where will the Yellow Cameras wander?

Each camera starts in one city with one person. From there, it’s up to the world: backpackers, commuters, musicians, families, night-shift workers, festival crowds, and quiet early-morning walkers. Pins on this map mark launch zones—but where the cameras end up is entirely up to the people who carry them.
